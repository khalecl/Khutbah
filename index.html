Welcome to Khutbah Live Translator

🌍
Choose Your Language
Select your preferred interface language. This changes the app menus and buttons to your language. You can change this later in Settings.
💡 You can also change the sermon translation languages separately in the Translator tab.
🕌
Welcome, Brother/Sister!
This app translates Friday sermons (Khutbah) in real-time, helping Muslims worldwide understand the beautiful teachings regardless of language barriers.
💡 Works in any mosque or while watching online sermons.
🌍
Select Languages
Choose the Source Language (what the Imam speaks) and Translation Language (your preferred language). Only verified, working language combinations are shown.
💡 Select a regional dialect (Saudi, Egyptian, etc.) for better speech recognition accuracy.
🎤
Start Listening
Tap the microphone button to start. The app listens and translates automatically. The audio visualizer shows when speech is detected.
💡 Position your phone near the speaker. Allow microphone permission when prompted.
📖
Quran & Du'a
Read Quran with Arabic text, transliteration, and translation in 30+ languages. Browse a collection of essential Du'as for daily life with meanings.
💡 Use the translation dropdown in the Quran tab to read in your own language!
📱
Cross-Platform
Android: Use Chrome browser. Keep screen on. iPhone/iPad: Use Safari browser. Add to Home Screen for best experience.
💡 The app uses dual translation engines for maximum reliability.
🤲
You're Ready!
May Allah bless you. This app is completely free and made with love for the Muslim Ummah. Please share it with others!

Khutbah Live Translator

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ
This application was created to help Muslims around the world understand Friday sermons regardless of language barriers. Whether you're a new Muslim, living abroad, or simply want to understand a sermon in a different language — this tool is for you.
v4.6 Features:
• Multi-language UI (English, Arabic, Urdu + more via lang/ files)
• Quran reader with 30+ translation languages
• Dual translation engine (Lingva + MyMemory fallback)
• Du'a collection with categories and references
• Prayer times with Qibla compass
• Offline caching for all content
• Cross-platform PWA (Android + iOS ready)
• Dark theme for mosque-friendly use
