Welcome to Khutbah Live Translator

🕌
Welcome, Brother/Sister!
This app translates Friday sermons (Khutbah) in real-time, helping Muslims worldwide understand the beautiful teachings regardless of language barriers.
💡 Tip: Works in any mosque or while watching online sermons.
🌍
Select Languages
Choose the Source Language (what the Imam speaks) and Translation Language (your preferred language). Only verified, working language combinations are shown.
💡 Tip: Select a regional dialect (Saudi, Egyptian, etc.) for better speech recognition accuracy.
🎤
Start Listening
Tap the microphone button to start. The app listens and translates automatically. The audio visualizer shows when speech is detected.
💡 Tip: Position your phone near the speaker. Allow microphone permission when prompted.
📖
Quran & Du'a
Read Quran with Arabic text, transliteration, and translation. Browse a collection of essential Du'as for daily life with meanings.
💡 Tip: Use the Quran tab to follow along during the khutbah when Imam recites verses.
📱
Cross-Platform
Android: Use Chrome browser. Keep screen on.

iPhone/iPad: Use Safari browser. Add to Home Screen for best experience.
💡 Tip: The app uses dual translation engines for maximum reliability.
🤲
You're Ready!
May Allah bless you. This app is completely free and made with love for the Muslim Ummah. Please share it with others!

This application was created to help Muslims around the world understand Friday sermons regardless of language barriers. Whether you're a new Muslim, living abroad, or simply want to understand a sermon in a different language — this tool is for you.
v4.4 Features:
• Dual translation engine (Lingva + MyMemory fallback)
• Quran reader with Arabic, transliteration & translation
• Du'a collection with categories and references
• Translation caching to reduce API usage
• Cross-platform PWA (Android + iOS ready)
• Dark theme for mosque-friendly use
• Android Chrome mic conflict fix (v4.2)
